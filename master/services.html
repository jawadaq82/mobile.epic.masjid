Full Time HIFZ School

DARUL QURAN FULL TIME HIFZ SCHOOL

By the blessing of ALLAH,EPIC opened the new Darul Quran Hifz School on Sep 4,2012. This school is running under the supervision of Hafiz Sajjad Gul and is a full time program (6-7 hrs per day) dedicated to helping students master the memorization and recitation of the Quran.The students must meet the selection criteria before admission. Homeschooling and the study of non-Quranic subjects should be arranged separately by parents.

Medical Clinic

The EPIC Medical Clinic provides basic primary medical care in internal medicine, family practice, general pediatrics and adolescent medicine.

NOTE: THIS SERVICES IS ONLY RESERVED FOR PEOPLE WHO DO NOT HAVE ANY KIND OF HEALTH INSURANCE AND WHO CANNOT AFFORT TO SEE A DOCTOR.

Medical Clinic is held every Saturday of the Month from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM

Note: There is NO CLINIC on the 5th Saturday.
